Web Development Skills

Backend Development

My degree in computer science gives me the foundation to complete complicated back-end work. I love the challenge of writing business logic for useful features that delight users. Reliable server-side code is the ultimate basis for a successful project, therefore I'm committed to continuously learning and using best practices.

Web Analytics

Analytics has intrigued me ever since I started developing websites for businesses.
